You need a workhorse drip coffee that keeps the brunch crowd happy, espresso that your baristas can pull consistently on the La Marzocco, and a cold brew that stands on its own.

Based on your setup, I'd recommend starting with **Summit Blend for your drip and espresso, plus Morning Trail for cold brew**. Here's why this will work for your customers:

- **Summit Blend** has chocolate and caramel notes that pair perfectly with breakfast and brunch menus - it's our most popular for a reason. It's also forgiving on both your Fetco batch brewer and the La Marzocco, so your baristas won't fight with it
- **Morning Trail** is bright and fruity - exactly what makes a cold brew stand out. Your customers will taste the difference from day one
- Both are smooth enough that the people on their third cup at 11am are still enjoying it
